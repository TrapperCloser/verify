Community Guidelines

Note: these rules are just guidelines and we reserve the right to take action if we believe the content you post violates community standards. The rules include but are not limited to:

  • No NSFW Content: Images and media containing extreme blood, gore, violence and/or adult content are strictly prohibited.
  • No Harmful Speech: Offensive text such as racial slurs and/or verbal harassment will result in a mute.
  • No Spam: Sending many messages in short periods of time and/or large text walls will result in a mute.
  • No Advertising: Promoting irrelevant links and/or other media will result in a mute.
  • No Exploiting: Circumventing punishments will result in more severe consequences, such as account suspension or an IP ban.

We thank you for respecting these guidelines and hope you have a positive experience.
